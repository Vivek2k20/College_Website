Facilities

BIT offers a lot of facilities like:

  • Auditorium
  • Well equiped Seminar Hall
  • Indoor Sports Auditorium
  • Alumni Association
  • Cooperative Society
  • Canteen

R&D Centres

Bangalore institute of technology has established recognized research and development (R & D) centres at all the thirteen departments.
These R & D centres offer full time and part time PhD and MSc (Engineering) programs for eligible research scholars.

Placement and Training Center

The Center provides employment opportunities for the students in acclaimed world class organisations. Every year more than 100 top notch companies visit our Institution and conduct placement activities for the full time recruitment and internship.
Leading companies like Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cisco, Akamai, Microfocus, HPE, SAP, Nokia, Microchip, BEL, Mercedes Benz, Bosch, L&T- ECC and Brigade Group recruit our students.

Library

The central library houses a total collection of 82992 documents and 19572 titles covering all the major fields of science and engineering. Central library is well equipped with latest edition of books, bound journals and E- journals, magazines, standards, CD's, project reports etc
The central library is housed in two separate wings of main building with a carpet area of over 1204sq mts and is located in the center of the campus.
