Facilities

BIT offers a lot of facilities like:

  • Training and Placement Center
  • State of the Art Library
  • Auditorium
  • Well equiped Seminar Hall
  • Indoor Sports Auditorium
  • Alumni Association
  • Cooperative Society
  • Canteen
  • Hostel

R&D Centres

Bangalore institute of technology has established recognized research and development (R & D) centres at all the thirteen departments.
These R & D centres offer full time and part time PhD and MSc (Engineering) programs for eligible research scholars.

Placement and Training Center

The Center provides employment opportunities for the students in acclaimed world class organisations. Every year more than 100 top notch companies visit our Institution and conduct placement activities for the full time recruitment and internship.
Leading companies like Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cisco, Akamai, Microfocus, HPE, SAP, Nokia, Microchip, BEL, Mercedes Benz, Bosch, L&T- ECC and Brigade Group recruit our students.

Library

The central library houses a total collection of 82992 documents and 19572 titles covering all the major fields of science and engineering. Central library is well equipped with latest edition of books, bound journals and E- journals, magazines, standards, CD's, project reports etc
The central library is housed in two separate wings of main building with a carpet area of over 1204sq mts and is located in the center of the campus.

Hostels

The college has comprehensive and hygiene hostel facilities for the students. We have two separate hostels for boys and girls. Our hostels provides a distinct advantage of befriending environment and we have a ragging free hostel campus. The hostels have generators, solar, library and dining halls with a modern kitchen. Hostels are under the rigorous supervision of wardens, assistant wardens, very well managed office and disciplined staff.

Sports

Department of Physical Education & Sports Science of BIT serves an integral part of student and staff life on campus. The aim is to provide a broad spectrum of sports, recreation and leisure activities for students, and staff Members.
The department is striving to provide services and programs that stimulate growth, development and retention of students in a contemporary and safe environment that develops fitness and wellness, social interaction and leadership opportunities.

Auditorium

Kuvempu Kalakshetra is an auditorium with the capacity to accommodate a guest list over a thousand guests. It can be used for social events, affiliation ceremonies, school events, corporate seminars, conferences, symposiums, plays, competitions, prize-distribution ceremonies, and more.
In addition to this,the college houses a seminar hall of 300 seating capacity.
