Clubs at BIT

BIT takes pride in having clubs that cater to every students needs and promotes extracurricular activities. The aim of these clubs is to enrich the social and cultural life on the campus.
Student Clubs are formed around academic and national themes to add to the rich mosaics of student life. Students who have common interest come together to form an interesting, creative and one of a kind club committed to traditional life for preservation of culture and so on. Cultural and Technical clubs play a vital role in the overall development of students mindset and skill set as they learn how to manage time, deal with responsibilities and juggle between activities and studies.
